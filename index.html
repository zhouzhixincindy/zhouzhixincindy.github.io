A developmental psychologist researching how children grow up, and grow up alongside AI.

Penn GSE M.S.Ed. → Incoming Ph.D., University of Louisville (Fall 2026)

I am a recent Master’s graduate in Interdisciplinary Studies of Human Development at the University of Pennsylvania, with research experience at HGSE. This fall, I will begin my Ph.D. in Psychological Sciences at the University of Louisville, advised by Prof. Judith Danovitch.

My research focuses on child–AI interaction, especially on children’s trust, perception, and social engagement with AI. What keeps me curious are simple questions: How can we make sure the next best AI model considers children, our future? When I become a mother, can I confidently give my children the AI companion to have them interact without fear and doubt? I want to make the answer yes.

Thanks for stopping by! Hope you enjoy glancing at who I am, and feel free to contact and drop a question. I’d love to hear from you : )
